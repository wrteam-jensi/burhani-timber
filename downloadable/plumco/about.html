Over 30+ Years Experience In Timber & Construction Materials

Burhani Timbers has been a trusted name in the timber and construction materials industry for over three decades. We specialize in providing high-quality timber products, scaffolding solutions, steel materials, and construction tools to meet the diverse needs of builders, contractors, and construction companies.

Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred supplier in the construction industry. We source the finest timber from sustainable forests and maintain strict quality control standards to ensure our products meet international quality benchmarks.

Ahmed Burhani

Burhani Timbers, Managing Director
